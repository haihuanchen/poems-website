Highest Truth, Greatest Love

BY Conversations with God

Be a living, breathing example of the Highest Truth that resides within you.
Speak humbly of yourself, so someone cannot mistake your Highest Truth for a boast.
Speak softly, so no one thinks you are merely calling for attention.
Speak gently, that all might know of Love.
Speak openly, so no one thinks you have something to hide.
Speak less often, so that your word may truly be taken seriously.
Speak respectfully, that no one is dishonored.
Speak lovingly, that every syllable may heal.
Speak of God with every utterance.
Be a gift to everyone who enters your life, and to everyone whose life you enter.
When someone enters your life unexpectedly, look for the gift that person has come to receive from you.
Every person come to you has come to receive a gift from you.
In so doing, he gives a gift to you - the gift of your experiencing and fulfilling Who You Are.
