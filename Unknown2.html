Strength of the Mountain

BY Unknown

The mountains in the heart of your soul
stand effortlessly through time.
Stationary are the fine peaks
of your noble grace.

The soul of time - strong.
fortified by experience and the weathering
of all that has stood in its way.

May you find the strength of the mountain
in the heart of your soul.
For it is there -
unmoving, unlimited
and with the face of time
etched in its memory.
